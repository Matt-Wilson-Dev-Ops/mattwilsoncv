Logan Safety Camera Program

Licence Plate Recognition Data

The company who designed the Video Monitoring Software used to monitor the LSCP cameras included the ability for certain cameras
to read and store information of licence plates passing through their FOV.

There was a significant issue in that this information was not retrievable by the monitoring officers
without technical assistance once a pre-determined period had passed.
In response to this need, I created a solution through a user friendly application accessing the database.

LPR Data Retrieval

The need to have this information at the fingertips of our staff was paramount. It enabled fast responses to QPS which increased their chances of apprehending suspect vehicles.

Utilising languages such as SQL, PHP, JS and HTML I designed a solution to allow anyone access to plates passing through cameras from any time period.
The challenge here was to enable this functionality without allowing users to directly interact with the database.

A very user friendly yet restricted and as a result safe application was conceived. Provided the staff member had the plate they would be able to see the cameras it passed through, the times down to the second and a rough frequency for its movements.

This project proved to be invaluable to the LSCP and QPS.
