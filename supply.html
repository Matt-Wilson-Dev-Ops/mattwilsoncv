Big Play Games
Supply Chain & Retail Infastructure

Implimenting a means to order, receive, stock, sell and restock from scratch
truly proved to be challenging without any prior infastructure or process.
Below is a snapshot of the systems before and after my employment at
Big Play Games on October 2019.

2017 - Oct 2019

Big Play Games had no means of tracking inventory, stock orders or managing cashflow during this period. You will notice there is not a continual flow of process, after a sale was completed plans for re-ordering stock would only occur in the event a staff member noticed it was required. Gross and net were never considered, only revenue.

Oct 2019 - Current

Every aspect of the business is now tied to a central application, DEAR Inventory. The accounting software, point of sale, shipping software and website all form an easy to trouble shoot and manage digital ecosystem with DEAR at it's core. A strict process is in place for every aspect of the supply chain ensuring preservation of efficieny and cost effective work practices.
